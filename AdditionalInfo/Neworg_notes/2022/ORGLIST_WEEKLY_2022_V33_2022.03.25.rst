AquariOS
===

AquariOS (Portmanteau for **Aquari**um **O**perating **S**ystem) is a small, basic, embedded operating system for fishtank/aquarium management. Based on TinyOS, and written in nesC and Verilog, AquariOS can help you automate some basic tasks of your aquarium. No Internet required for anything.

Features:

Change the brightness/lighting based on the time of day (including but not limited to: brightness level, color, color range, pattern of colors, pattern of brightness, frequency)

Automatically feed the fish at certain times of the day, also allows for specifying which fish food to place in

Operate a tiny operating system (system requirements: 4 megabytes of RAM, 64 megabytes disk space)
